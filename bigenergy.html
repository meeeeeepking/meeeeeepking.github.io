Big Energy

About

Big Energy is a game about green energy produciton The game has you try to meet ever growing energy demands while minimizing the money spent and the damage to the enviorment. the main goal of the game was to emphasize the importance of nuclear fusion technology in the future.

since this game was made for a capstone about clean energy, the stats of the different buildings are based off of real life statistics and each building contains information about that form of energy production.

Gameplay

The game takes place in 10 turns each representing 10 years. on each turn you must produce enough energy to keep up with demand while trying to not spend too much money or cause too much damage to the enviorment. You are able to produce energy through various means from coal to solar to eventualy nuclear fusion. At the end of the 10 turns, you will be given a score breakdown showing how well you preformed.

Gameplay vs Realism

A big struggle durring this project was deciding on the stats of the various buildings since I wanted them to somewhat relate to real life statistics but I also needed to make the game balanced and fun. A big part of this struggle came from the size difference between the different types of power generation since most of the green energy producers take up orders of magnitude more space than fossil fuels. In the end the size differences had to be scaled down but the difference is still enough to be felt especialy with the little non forested land the player is given.

What I Learned

the main new thing i learned through this project is the use of tile sets and tile maps as I had never touched them prior to this project.
